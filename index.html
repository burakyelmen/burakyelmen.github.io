Research Overview

We investigate human genomic variation from functional and evolutionary perspectives through computational modeling. Our research focuses on developing predictive and generative neural networks to model genomic data in relation to complex traits and other omics layers. We also develop interpretability methods for deep learning models to generate biological insight.
